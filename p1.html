Big data is a term used for data sets whose size or type extends beyond the capability of traditional relational databases to capture, manage and process low latency data.
Big data has one or more features: high volume, high velocity or high variety.
Data sophistication is powered by artificial intelligence ( AI), web, social and the Internet of Things ( IoT).Drives data complexity by means of new forms and data sources.

Big-data analysis helps analysts, researchers and business users to make smarter and quicker decisions using previously unavailable or unusable data.
Businesses can use advanced analytical techniques such as text analysis, machine learning, predictive analysis , data mining, statistics and the processing of natural languages to gain new insights from previously untapped data sources independently or together with existing enterprise data
Big data analytics is the use of advanced analytical techniques against very large , diverse data sets that include structured, semi-structured and unstructured data from different sources, from terabytes to zetta bytes, in different sizes
